About Me

Hi, my name is Adiba and I started The Board Bae ATX to combine my love of hosting and Charcuterie. I believe all you need to elevate your hosting game from simple to wow-worthy spreads is great presentation skills. And some delicious cheese of course! You don't need Artisan noms, a quick trip to TJ's and you're set to wow your guests if you know what to buy :) So follow along while I share tips and tricks along with fantastic boards/grazing table inspiration for everyone!
