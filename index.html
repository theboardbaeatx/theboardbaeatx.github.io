Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is a cheeseboard?
A cheeseboard is a platter or board that is used to serve a variety of cheeses, along with accompaniments such as crackers, fruits, and nuts.
What types of cheese are typically included in a cheeseboard?
A cheeseboard can include a variety of cheeses such as cheddar, brie, gouda, blue cheese, and goat cheese.
Do you offer customizable cheeseboards?
Yes, we offer customizable cheeseboards where you can choose the types of cheese and accompaniments you prefer.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or preferences?
Yes, we can accommodate dietary restrictions or preferences. Please let us know your requirements when placing your order.
Do you provide delivery services?
Yes, we provide delivery services within the Austin area. Delivery charges may apply.
